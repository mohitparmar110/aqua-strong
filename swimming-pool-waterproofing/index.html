Why Pool Waterproofing Is Critical

Pool structures face constant water pressure and chemical exposure. If waterproofing fails, leakage can spread behind tiles, weaken the concrete, and increase maintenance costs.

Quick Note: If your pool water level drops without obvious evaporation, it often indicates leakage through cracks, tile joints, or fittings.

Common Signs You Need Pool Waterproofing

Sign What It Typically Means
Unusual water loss Leakage through cracks, joints, or fittings
Hollow/loose tiles Water migration behind tiles weakening adhesion
White deposits (efflorescence) Moisture movement carrying salts to the surface
Cracks in pool shell Structural movement or shrinkage creating leak paths
